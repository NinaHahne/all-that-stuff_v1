Instructions:

If it's your turn to build:

  • drag&drop objects from the right to the construction area in the middle
  • double-click objects to switch perspective (only for some objects)
  • press Q or E to rotate the last clicked object in the construction area
  • click done! when you're finished building

If you have to guess:

  • wait for the builder to finish and then click on a word on the left side to make your guess

Sound settings:

  • press S to toggle between two sound modes (universal vs. object specific)
  • press M to mute sounds
